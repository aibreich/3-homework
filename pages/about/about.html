Giving People Cars

We started giving away manual cars a few years ago to some of our supporters, through a series of "Save The Manuals" giveaways. We wanted to give other people the chance to experience what high performance cars feel like--a chance some people may never get.

After recent events, we realized that modified cars, and even the ability to modify YOUR OWN CAR, are in danger thanks to new, tighter restrictions being enforced by the EPA-doesn't matter if you want to turn your personal property into a drag monster that's built only for the track. Ridiculous right? We agree.

A few organizations and other creators on the internet are trying to do something about it, by pushing and promoting the RPM Act.

What is the RPM Act? Well, from SEMA: "The RPM Act of 2021 (H.R. 3281), is common-sense, bi-partisan legislation to protect Americans' right to convert street vehicles into dedicated racecars and the motorsports-parts industry's ability to sell products that enable racers to compete."

We stand for this, and we want to do our part. Aside from promoting this on YouTube, we're shifting the direction and goal of our giveaways. Manual cars may be an endangered species, but now things are getting a little more serious--ALL RACECARS now have a target on their back. If we're going to get people's attention from what we're doing, we want them to be aware of what's going on, and what they can do about it.
